BizAI Demo Company Policies

1. Leave Policy
- Full-time employees receive 18 days of annual paid leave per calendar year.
- Casual leave is limited to 6 days per year.
- Sick leave is limited to 10 days per year.
- Any leave longer than 3 consecutive working days requires manager approval.
- Carry-forward of annual leave is capped at 5 days into the next year.

2. Work From Home Policy
- Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
- Staff handling confidential finance, payroll, or legal operations must use company-issued laptops only.
- Public Wi-Fi may not be used for company systems unless connected through company VPN.

3. Expense Reimbursement Policy
- Meals during approved business travel are reimbursable up to $40 per day.
- Hotel stays are reimbursable up to $140 per night unless pre-approved by Finance.
- Taxi and rideshare claims require receipts for any single trip above $15.
- Reimbursement claims must be submitted within 21 days of the expense date.

4. Procurement Policy
- Purchases under $500 may be approved by a department manager.
- Purchases between $500 and $2,500 require both manager and Finance approval.
- Purchases above $2,500 require Procurement review and one executive approver.
- Any software subscription handling customer data must be reviewed by Security before purchase.

5. Information Security Policy
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters long.
- Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all staff accounts.
- Customer data may not be downloaded to personal devices.
- Suspected phishing must be reported to security@demo-bizai.local within 1 hour.

6. Travel Policy
- International travel requires approval from the department head at least 7 business days in advance.
- Economy class is required for flights shorter than 6 hours.
- Business class may be booked only for flights longer than 6 hours with executive approval.

7. Performance Review Policy
- Formal performance reviews occur twice per year in June and December.
- Managers must submit review summaries within 5 business days of the review meeting.

8. Vendor Management Policy
- New vendors must sign the standard data processing agreement if they process customer or employee data.
- Critical vendors must be reviewed annually by Legal and Security.

9. Hiring Policy
- Open roles must have an approved job description and salary band before posting.
- At least two interviewers must independently score final-round candidates.

10. Escalation Contacts
- HR questions: hr@demo-bizai.local
- Finance reimbursement questions: finance@demo-bizai.local
- Security incidents: security@demo-bizai.local
